Research

From nanobubbles to spatial transcriptomics — exploring what imaging and data can reveal about the human body.

Penn State BASiL Lab · Summer 2025 · AHA-Sponsored

High-Frame-Rate Doppler Ultrasound for Deep Vein Thrombosis Delineation

Developed a MATLAB-based imaging pipeline to resolve clot morphology and flow patterns invisible to standard B-mode imaging. Redesigned 3D-printed clot phantoms and benchtop flow rigs, achieving ~40% improvement in clot boundary clarity. First author and presenter at BMES 2025 (San Diego). Work selected by the Washington University Journal of Undergraduate Research.

MATLAB · Verasonics Medical Ultrasound Signal Processing First Author · BMES 2025

WashU Chen Ultrasound Lab · Nov 2024 – Present

Nanobubble Contrast Agents for Cerebrospinal Fluid Imaging

Optimizing lipid-shell nanobubbles for echogenicity and stability in CSF imaging. Investigating ultrasound neuromodulation to induce hibernation-like states for critical care applications.

Nanobubbles Ultrasound Imaging Neuromodulation

WashU Ding Lab (School of Medicine) · Mar 2024 – Jan 2025

Tumor Microenvironment via Spatial Transcriptomics

Applied single-cell sequencing to 100+ samples (colon, myeloma, ccRCC, breast). Identified 10+ novel therapeutic biomarkers across tumor clusters using R programming, contributing to targeted cancer therapy development.

Spatial Transcriptomics R Programming Cancer Genomics
