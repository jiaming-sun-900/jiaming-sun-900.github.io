About Me

Beyond the lab and the lecture hall.

Music

Electric guitarist since 2020. Co-founded The Half-Body Harp, a blues rock band at WashU. I arrange covers and write original pieces blending classic rock with acoustic textures. We've headlined two independent concerts.

Aviation

Aviation enthusiast with a particular soft spot for the Airbus A330neo. I find the engineering behind high-bypass turbofans and composite airframes oddly similar to the precision needed in medical device design.

Background

Born and raised in Beijing, attended RDFZ high school. Now in St. Louis on an F-1 visa. Fluent in Mandarin and English, independently learning Japanese. Proud to bridge two very different engineering cultures.

Beyond the Lab

I produced a video essay on Chinese cinema (Jiang Wen and Peter Chan) and a podcast on urban landscapes in Beijing films. I find the overlap between culture, place, and human experience just as fascinating as science.
